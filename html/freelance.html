Composer, Bandleader, Freelancer

Overview

Since the age of 16, Michael has been an active freelance musician in Washington, D.C. In December, 2018, his trio, Superstring and the Cosmic Vibrations, released their first album which can be previewed below:

Superstring are in post-production of their second album and look forward to performing live again after the mandatory "pandemic hiatus" as it gradually becomes more safe to gather again.

Michael is additionally the co-leader of the KW Big Band alongside pianist and composer Tim Whalen.

Check out Tim's Acetone Sessions interview with Michael for more information about Michael's musical upbringing, the recording of the second Superstring record and the KW Big Band.

Michael has additionally cultivated a reputation as an in demand side man. In November 2020, he joined saxophonist and bandleader Ricky Parrell for a meaningful and socially distant studio recording project "Mourning Summer", Ricky's musical reflection on the first summer of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Other notable sideman projects include saxophonist Joseph Henson's 2019 album "Transformation"
