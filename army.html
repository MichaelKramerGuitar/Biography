Army Career

Michael won his job at the U.S. Army Band "Pershing's Own", U.S. Army Blues in the Summer of 2013 while he was stationed at Navy Band Northwest in Kitsap County, Bangor WA. Michael's responsibilities in the Army Band include:

  • Guitar
  • Banjo
  • Dobro
  • Logistics
He is a regularly featured soloist with The Army Blues and is the Senior Producer for the Popular Music Element.
